👋 Welcome to Whatdyawant?

This app helps you create, share, and claim gifts from wishlists with friends. Your wishlist is shared with whomever you chose, they are able to save your wishlist and see updates. You are unable to see if anybody has claimed your gifts and any claims are anonymous!

Latest Features:

  • 🛍️ Add gift ideas to your shopping list
  • ✅ Filters and Sort
  • 🎁 Wishlists can now be viewed by logged out guest users
  • 🔎 Guest users will not be able to see/make claims without signing in
  • 💾 Save a wishlist link you were sent

Coming soon:

  • 🔗 Autofill product details from pasted URLs
  • 🖼️ Wishlist cover images and item photos
  • 📱 Improved mobile experience
  • 🔎 See gift ideas in list view
